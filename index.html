About Elite Groovers

More than music — a sound, a culture, an energy.

Elite Groovers prove that brass can lead, not just support. What started in Takoradi as a small group of friends has grown into nearly 80 musicians. With soaring brass, driving percussion, and Ghanaian rhythms, the band creates performances that stay with you long after the last note.

From local weddings to international festivals, Elite Groovers transform stages into celebrations — culture, joy, and community carried through music.

Founder — Alex Halm

Vision & Leadership

Alex Halm founded Elite Groovers with one belief: brass can carry the story. From that vision grew a band known for its powerful sound, flawless performances, and a community that keeps growing with every show.
