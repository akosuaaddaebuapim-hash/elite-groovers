About Elite Groovers

More than music. A sound, a culture, an energy.

Elite Groovers prove that brass can lead. What began in Takoradi with a handful of friends has grown into an ensemble of nearly eighty musicians. Their horns soar, percussion drives, and their shows turn moments into memory.

Weddings, parades, festivals, or international stages, Elite Groovers bring music that moves people. Their sound blends bold brass and Ghanaian rhythm into a single pulse.

“Music lifts what words cannot hold.”

Founder Alex Halm

Vision and leadership

Alex started Elite Groovers with a simple idea: let brass tell the story. From that belief he built a band that sounds bold, tight, and joyful. Each show reflects a community that keeps growing.

“The sound of many becomes the voice of one.”
