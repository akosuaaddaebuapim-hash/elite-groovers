About Us

More than music — a sound, a culture, an energy.

Elite Groovers began in Takoradi with a handful of friends and a vision: to let brass carry the story. Today the band is nearly eighty strong, known for bold horns, driving rhythms, and performances that turn stages into celebrations.

“Where words fail, music speaks.”

Founder Alex Halm

Alex Halm founded Elite Groovers with one belief: brass can lead, not follow. His vision shaped a band that carries Ghanaian culture to the world stage, building unity through sound.

“The sound of a brass band is the sound of unity.”
