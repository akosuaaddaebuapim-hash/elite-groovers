About

Elite Groovers Brass Band was founded in Takoradi, Ghana, in 2020 by Alex Halm. What began as a small group of passionate musicians has now grown into one of the largest brass bands in Ghana, with over 70 members today.

The band is built around a unique sound: trumpets and horns leading the melody, supported by drums, keyboards, and bass. Their goal has always been simple – to bring joy, rhythm, and a true Ghanaian spirit to every performance.

Elite Groovers play at weddings, festivals, concerts, and major events, and have also collaborated with artists across Ghana, blending their brass driven style with modern afrobeat and contemporary music.

Their mission is clear: to take Ghana’s brass tradition to the world stage.
