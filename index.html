About

Elite Groovers began in Takoradi, Ghana in 2020 when Alex Halm gathered a group of music lovers around one idea: that trumpets and horns could lead the melody, not just support it. From those early days the band has grown to nearly 80 members, filling weddings, festivals and concerts with energy, rhythm and a sound that is proudly Ghanaian.

Meet the Founder

Alex Halm founded Elite Groovers with a vision: to create a band where brass takes center stage. His leadership has guided the group from a small circle of players to one of Ghana’s largest brass ensembles.

What We Do

From weddings and festivals to collaborations with top Ghanaian artists, Elite Groovers bring brass, rhythm, and unforgettable energy to every event.
