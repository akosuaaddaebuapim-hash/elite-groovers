About Elite Groovers

More than music. A sound shaped by brass and rhythm.

Elite Groovers began in Takoradi with a small group of friends and a single idea: let brass carry the story. The band grew into a large ensemble that blends horn melody, percussion, and Ghanaian groove into performances that feel like celebration.

They play parades, festivals, weddings and global stages. The sound is big, warm, and alive.

“Music moves what words cannot hold.”

Founder Alex Halm

Vision, craft, community.

Leadership in sound

Alex formed the band with a belief that brass can lead the conversation. He built a culture that values precision, joy, and performance. Under his direction the band grew and began to travel beyond Ghana to share the groove.

“The sound of many becomes the voice of one.”
