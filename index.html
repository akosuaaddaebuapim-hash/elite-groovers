About Elite Groovers

Where brass meets heartbeat and culture turns into sound.

Elite Groovers are proof that brass can lead. Born in Takoradi as a handful of passionate players, the band has grown into an 80-strong powerhouse. Their horns soar, drums thunder, and together they turn every performance into a celebration.

From weddings and parades to international stages, Elite Groovers embody joy, culture, and energy that unites audiences across the world.

Founder — Alex Halm

Vision & Leadership

Alex Halm founded Elite Groovers with one belief: brass should carry the story. Guided by this vision, the band grew from a close circle of friends into Ghana’s leading brass ensemble, known for world-class sound and unforgettable performances.
