About Us

Elite Groovers is a brass band with a big sound and a bigger spirit. Born in Takoradi, Ghana, we began as a handful of friends playing for the love of music. Today we are nearly eighty strong and our music travels far beyond our hometown.

Our horns carry the melody, our drums drive the heartbeat, and together we bring an energy that lifts every stage. From local celebrations to international festivals, Elite Groovers make every moment unforgettable.

Founder

Alex Halm founded Elite Groovers with a vision to let brass instruments lead the sound—building a band that grew from a small group in Takoradi to an internationally recognized ensemble.
