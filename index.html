About Elite Groovers

Short, powerful, and real. Let the music (and visuals) speak.

Elite Groovers is proof that brass can lead, not just follow. What began in Takoradi with a small circle of passionate players has grown into a powerful ensemble of nearly 80 musicians, blending rich brass melodies, Ghanaian rhythm, and unstoppable energy.

On every stage they don’t just play songs — they create an experience. Brass carries the melody, percussion drives the heartbeat, and together they turn moments into memories at weddings, festivals, and international shows.

From the first note to the last, Elite Groovers stands for culture, joy, and a sound that moves people.

Founder — Alex Halm

Vision & Leadership

Alex started Elite Groovers with a simple belief: brass can lead the story. Under his direction the band grew from a handful of friends into one of Ghana’s most exciting ensembles, known for big sound, tight performances, and a culture that brings people together.
