About Elite Groovers

More than music a sound shaped by brass and rhythm

Elite Groovers started in Takoradi with a small circle of friends and a single idea. Let brass carry the story. Today the band is nearly eighty strong and brings stage ready energy to weddings parades festivals and international shows.

“Where words fail music speaks”

Founder Alex Halm

Vision craft community

Leadership in sound

Alex founded the band with one clear idea let brass lead the conversation. His vision turned a local group into a tight ensemble that plays with joy precision and heart.
